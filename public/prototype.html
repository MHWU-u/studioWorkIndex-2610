Emoji Landscape Prototype

This prototype expands the earlier emoji study into a scene-making tool. It keeps the simple grid system, but adds more natural and urban elements so users can build small landscapes and city compositions more easily.

What this version tests

Expanded visual vocabulary: more natural and city emojis support scene-building rather than only isolated symbols.

Category-based generation: users can switch between different image sets depending on the type of composition they want to make.

Mapping + feedback: changes in scale, rotation, and mirroring appear immediately in the preview and on the grid.

Simple world-building: the stage themes help create different moods such as sky, night, or concrete urban space.

This version keeps the project limited in scope, but makes the tool more useful for building small landscapes, mixed environments, and symbolic city scenes.
